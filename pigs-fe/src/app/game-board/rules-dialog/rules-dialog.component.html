Gameplay:

Players simultaneously choose one card from their hand to play face-down.

Once all players have chosen a card, reveal them simultaneously. Starting with the player who played the lowest card (in numerical order), each player places their card onto one of the four rows on the table. The card must be placed in the row with the highest card that is lower than the card being played.

If a player's card would be the sixth card in a row (thus causing them to take the row), they must take the row and all the cards in it. The player's card becomes the start of a new row.

Scoring:

Each bullhead card (cards with numbers) taken by a player counts as negative points. The player with the fewest bullheads at the end of the game wins.
